PB&J Surprise

Description

This wonderful snack is the perfect close to any night of debauchery. Lightly toasted white bread, smoooooth peanut butter, and a tantalizing strawberry jelly; this PBJ is like no other.

"But wait..." you may be wondering, "that sounds exactly like a regular old PB&J like mom used to make." Well you're wrong. Because in THIS PB&J there lies a surprise. Smack dab in the center of this sandwhich there is a single banana slice. Placed delicately amidst the swirls of red jelly and brown peanut butter, this scrumptious piece of banana will really knock your socks off! Let's get started.

Ingredients

  • Bread, white
  • Peanut butter, smooth
  • Jelly, strawberry
  • Banana, sliced

Steps

  1. Lightly toast 2 slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on 1 slice of toast
  3. Spread jelly on other slice of toast
  4. Slice banana (be sure to use the same knife!)
  5. Place single banana slice in center of peanut butter
  6. Carefully combine both halves of sandwhich into single unit
  7. EAT THAT THANG!
